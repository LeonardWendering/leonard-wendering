Leonard Wendering

PhD Student in Sociology, University of Mannheim (since 2023)

My research focuses on intergroup relations, prejudice reduction, and social cohesion. I employ (quasi-)experimental methods both in the field and on large-scale data. In my current projects, I investigate interethnic exposure effects in childhood on later-life marriage choices, contact theory in a vocational school setting, and AI's impact on academic work.

Currently, I am preparing four working papers. I present my work at multiple international conferences including IMEBESS 2024 and ACES 2024, with upcoming presentations at ISA 2025 and ECSR 2025.
